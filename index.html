About Me

MY STORY

I'm Ashutosh Omane, a video editor specializing in long-form storytelling, short-form content, motion graphics, and AI-assisted creative workflows.

My fascination with video production began from a curiosity about how stories are crafted through editing. What started as an interest gradually evolved into a focused pursuit of visual storytelling and content creation.

My background in graphic design helped me develop strong visual communication skills, while years spent studying financial markets strengthened my analytical thinking, attention to detail, and problem-solving abilities.

Today, I combine design principles, storytelling, motion graphics, and modern AI tools to create engaging content for creators, brands, and digital platforms.

Based in Pune, Pimpri-Chinchwad, Maharashtra and available for on-site, hybrid, and remote opportunities.
